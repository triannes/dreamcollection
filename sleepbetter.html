What makes YOU sleep better?

Something sweet to eat

A piece of dark chocolate, bananas, almonds, pistachios, peanuts, and cherries at least an hour before bed can help you sleep better! At least that's what experts say. It's always good to end your day on a sweet note to wash away any bitter experiences before heading to sleep.

Something soft to sleep on

After a long day, your bed is there to guide you to sweet dreams. Rest your head on a soft fluffed up pillow and head to slumberville. Although, soft matresses are better for side sleepers.

Something fluffy to hug

Grab your furry friend, a body pillow, or plushies. If you have none of those, hug the fluffiest blanket you have and wear the plushiest cloud-like pajamas and drift off to sleep. Or would wishing on a star help you sleep better?

Something warm to calm the mind

Drinking chamomile, green tea, or no to low caffeine teas can help you calm down and relax your mind before you sleep. Be sure to drink tea at least 30 minutes before bed otherwise, you might have to take some trips before bedtime. Doesn't drinking tea make you feel all warm and cozy inside? It sometimes feels like a hug!
